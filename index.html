Welcome to WeastZaga'08 Crew

DWEASTZAGA is an independent registered youth group and nonprofit civil society organization based in Nairobi county. WEASTZAGA prime motto is capacity building and services delivery to the deprived and marginalized people of the society.

In 2014 a group of educated, dynamic and like-minded youth working in the development sector, got together to establish an organization, for addressing the most urgent and pressing needs of the people of KIBERA. True to this spirit the group was named weast and east gonzaga (WEASTZAGA).

Frequently Asked Question

01. What do we do?

Influenced by the new development paradigm, which puts people before things, weastzaga follows a participatory approach in every sphere of its work. The traditional knowledge of the slum’s poor is valued and respected, and there is confidence that slums people are capable of self-reliant organization. On a practical level, the fundamental thrust of this approach is decentralization and empowerment. For a community to be able to work for its own development and reduce its dependency there is a need for full involvement at all stages of the development cycle. The participation of communities in identification, planning, management, implementation and evaluation of projects is a key factor in Weastzaga work. Internally a participatory approach is adopted in managing the affairs of Weastzaga.

02. Our role in the society?

Externally, Weastzaga role as a “change agent” helps to develop community management structures that are capable of managing their own development. This increases the ability of the community to demand accountability in all their affairs, from leaders and officials. It reinforces the community's belief in the value of their own development choices and efforts. These community management structures are not parallel structures, but are built upon traditional institutions, promoting equitable representation and participation

03. Do we offer training to the society?

Human resource development is an essential part of our development work. This means ensuring that the community is trained and skilled, as well as organized, to maximize the potential of the community to take hold of its own development.

04. What are our believes?

Weastzaga strongly believes in maximizing its impact by transferring field experiences into knowledge to influence the development policies and practices of NGOs, donors
